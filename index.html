Innovative Electric Motorcycles

Yatri Motorcycles offers cutting-edge electric bikes like Project Zero and Project One, featuring sleek designs, impressive range, and advanced connectivity, perfect for both urban commuting and high-performance rides.

Advanced Battery Technology

Yatri Motorcycles use cutting-edge Lithium-Nickel-Manganese battery cells for enhanced energy density and efficiency. The battery pack in Project Zero holds 8 kWh of energy, while Project One features a 3 kWh battery, both offering rapid charging capabilities and long-lasting performance​.

Comprehensive Connectivity and Support

Yatri Energy provides a network of fast-charging stations and a subscription plan to make charging hassle-free and economical.
